\n It's soon to come from Kieron Gillen's, the creator of the Die comic. Dunno how many here are into comics but he does a great work on this one in terms of rpg experiences seen through time and age. The pitch for the comic at the Image page (the publishers) goes like this:

DIE is a pitch-black fantasy where a group of forty-something adults have to deal with the returning unearthly horror they barely survived as teenage role-players. If Kieron\u2019s in a rush, he describes it as \u201cGoth Jumanji.\u201d That only captures a sliver of what you\u2019ll find in oversized debut issue\u2014where fantasy gets all too real.

Here's an article in Polygon about it.

\n After a long, long break from my blog (seriously, it was a long time), today I give you not one, but two new short games!

Unwritten Lore is inspired by the expository cut-scenes in computer & console games and lets you replicate the experience of learning a new setting's lore by playing through it.

Tech Support II: Customer Returns is a pseudo-sequel to my 2014 game Tech Support and continues the same theme of customer & provider interactions involving strange products.

Please enjoy!

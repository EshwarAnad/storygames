    \n I could see MF0 reskinned to treat cars (and trucks and such) as 'frames'. I mean, you can abstract away the entire frame model and just use a stack of color-coded 2x2 bricks, iif you wanted to do so. What you WOULD lose from Car Wars is those hilarious, inadvertent losses of control or overspeed turns and collisions with terrain: there's nothing really in MF0 to simulate such continuous velocity and driver skill systems (they just walk; cannot even be tripped). MF0 also does not support anything like deployed countermeasures (oil slicks, spike strips). But then again, it doesn't support overheating systems like MechWarrior: it's supposed to be super lean and visually engaging, not High Simulation.

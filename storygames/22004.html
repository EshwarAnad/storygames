\n Hey all,

I\u2019m setting up a Patreon for zine that collects games, hacks, poems, LARPs, etc. that were previously unavailable in English. Smaller stuff is preferred since I\u2019ll be aiming for a 32 page, B&W, digest sized zine I think and quick turn around would keep everyone involved from burn out.

I\u2019m looking for an army of translators active in game scenes that can pitch me games they\u2019d like to see translated so I can pay them to do that. I don\u2019t mind helping with editing and localizing so you\u2019d by no means have to be a professional translator.

I\u2019d pay translators minimum 8 cents a word, maybe a negotiated flat fee for a game poem or more cents for something super grueling and/or technical.

The original authors would get a royalty cut from copies sold in perpetuity. I\u2019m thinking 2% per page taken up by their work in the zine, with a min of 2%.

If you or anyone you know is interested in being a translator who can approach game authors to get their works translated for me I\u2019d love to hear from you! My email is samjokopublishing@gmail.com, twitter handle is kylejsimons.

I\u2019ve also been trying to think of a cool name for the zine so any ideas here would be helpful as well! I think a one-word name would be best.

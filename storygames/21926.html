\n Does exactly what it says on the tin... basically, me and my housemate pick some games from Epistolary Richard's Indie RPG Pipeline and say what we think about them based on the blurbs.

Future editions, if we make them, will involve us buying and reading at least one of the games and possibly even playing it if we can find the time.

Here's our pilot podcast anyway.

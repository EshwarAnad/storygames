\n There's about 48 hours left on the Flotsam: Adrift Amongst the Stars Kickstarter. It's already funded, with over 500 backers and stretch goals by Emily Care Boss and Grant Howitt already in the bag.

Flotsam is a GMless story game where you play outcasts, misfits and renegades living in the belly of a space station, in the shadow of a more prosperous society. You play through their everyday lives, their interpersonal relationships and small-scale drama against the epic backdrop of space.

Imagine the Belters of the Expanse watching as Earth and Mars shape their lives, the civilians in Battlestar Galactica living with the decisions made by the military and the folk of Downbelow in Babylon 5, abandoned to destitution and squalor by those who built the station. This game is about characters like that.

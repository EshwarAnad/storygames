\n After a couple of expeditions in Antarctica, I started reading more about the past endeavors in the exploration of that wonderful continent. After some interesting findings, I wrote down this very simple game for 4-6 players about being stranded and facing certain death at the South Pole.
https://starfishgames.itch.io/hearts-in-antarctica-scott-edition
Would you be interested in reading it?

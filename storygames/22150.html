\u2022 DayTrippers, an open-ended, multidimensional system

\u2022 Why Tod isn\u2019t into Cyberpunk
\u2022 Snowcrash and postmodernism
\u2022 Capitalist realism
\u2022 Fractopian principles

\u2022 Fractopia podcast
